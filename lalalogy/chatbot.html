What is Lala chatbot?

Lala Chat bot often referred to as “Chat Robot” are AI (artificial Intelligence)- Driven programs through which any user can interact via Text or Voice interfaces. Lala works as a personal assistant and lives in many major chat applications like Facebook Messenger, Slack, Telegram, Imessage etc…

How does it work?

With the use of algorithm of Natural Language Processing (NLP) from Rasa enables Lala to acquire meaning from inputs given by users.
Lala assesses the intent of the input message from the users and then creates responses based on contextual analysis…similar to the way a human thinks.
